stimes2binraster

Calculate binraster from spike times.

Syntax

binraster = stimes2binraster(stimes,time,dt,ev_windows,window_margin)

Description

binraster = stimes2binraster(stimes,time,dt,ev_windows,window_margin) calculates binrasters using spike times (stimes) and time vector (time) at a resolution of dt. Time window boundaries corresponding to the rows of the binraster are specified by ev_windows. A margin of window_margin is added.
